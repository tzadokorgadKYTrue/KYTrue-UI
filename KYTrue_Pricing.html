FAQ
Common questions
What counts as a session?
Each time a user initiates a verification flow. KYC, KYB, insurance, or any other; that's one session token. Sandbox sessions are always free.
What's the re-verify discount?
If a user's Behavioural Identity Token already exists in our registry, re-verifying them costs only 0.3 tokens instead of 1.0. Their biometric fingerprint is already on record.
Can I switch plans mid-month?
Yes. You can upgrade instantly. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle. Annual plans can be upgraded at any time, prorated.
Who owns the Identity Tokens?
KYTrue maintains the Identity Registry. Clients receive the LiveScore and verification result via API, the underlying biometric model stays with us and is never exportable.
Is there a free trial for Growth?
Yes, 14 days free, no credit card required. You keep your sandbox data when you upgrade.
What happens if I exceed my quota?
Sessions continue uninterrupted. You'll be billed at the overage rate at the end of the month. Enterprise clients can set a hard cap if preferred.
